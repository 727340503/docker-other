Why should I create an account on Rustan’s Supermarket App?
  • With the new Stick ‘N Collect app, you can earn and share stickers, as well as redeem and reserve rewards using your mobile device. You will also be updated on the latest news and promos from us.
What do I need to create an account on Stick ‘N Collect?
  • All you need is to provide your first name, last name, and mobile number or email address. No worries, we promise to keep all the information that you share safe.
Why do you need those info?
  • This is our way of knowing and connecting with you better. In this way, we will be able to give you offers that will best suit you.
Can I share my Stick ‘N Collect app account with others?
  • Sharing your account is within your own discretion but we do not recommend it. However, you are encouraged to share stickers with your friends and family who are also using the app.
During registration, my Stick ‘N Collect app closed unexpectedly and I was not able to complete the registration process. What should I do?
  • Upon creation of account, you will receive a confirmation email. If you have not received any, you may try to register again.
I forgot my Stick ‘N Collect app password and tried to reset it using the “Forgot Password” function on Login page. However, I did not receive any email or instructions. Are there other ways to reset it?
  • You can try to use the “Forget password” function maximum 3 times per day. If you are still not able to receive any email instructions, contact us at stickers@rsci.com.ph.
How can I deactivate my Stick ‘N Collect app account? Can I get my account if it has been deactivated?
  • Shoot us an email at stickers@rsci.com.ph to inform us that you no longer want to use your account. Take note that if you have decided to deactivate your account, all your personal information will be deleted on our data base.
Can I log into Stick ‘N Collect app on multiple devices at the same time?
  • Unfortunately no, you can only log in one device at a time.
What device does Stick ‘N Collect app support?
  • It works well with both iOS (9.0 and below) and Android (5.0 and up).
Can I switch it off the app push notifications?
  • Yes, just go to the app's settings.
What should I do if I lose my mobile device, which had Stick ‘N Collect app installed?
  • If you have lost your mobile device, we recommend you to report immediately. Email us at stickers@rsci.com.ph to avoid the misuse of your earned stickers. You may want to log in to your account using another device to change your password.
Will I receive notifications about my sticker transactions e.g. sticker transfer, sticker redeemed, sticker earned?
  • For all transactions, upon completion, a pop up notification will appear. For “Email Verification” and “Forget Password”, an email notification would be sent to your registered email.
Can I share my stickers to others?
  • Of course! Stickers can be shared and transferred among app users. Before transferring, you have to add the recipient to your app’s friend list. The recipient will receive a push notification when stickers are transferred to his/her account. Sticker transfer only happens after user log in.
  • Email us at stickers@rsci.com.ph, we’d be happy to guide your through the process.
I sent some stickers to my friend but they didn’t receive any notification about it. What should I do?
  • Sticker transfer will normally be reflected within a few minutes. If the receiver did not receive any notifications, please make sure both of your devices are connected to a valid mobile or Wi-Fi network and the push notification for the app is enabled.
What should I do if I transferred stickers to the wrong account?
  • You should record down the wrong account and contact us immediately at stickers@rsci.com.ph for support.
Can I combine my manual stickers with digital stickers to redeem rewards?
  • No, stickers are strictly non-interchangeable and cannot be combined.
I would like to redeem a reward. What do I need to do?
  • You can go to the Reward page to choose the reward you want. You can either choose to redeem for free or thru fast track. When you have decided on what you want to redeem and clicked the “redeem” button, a unique redemption code will be generated. When you go to the store to get the reward, look for the customer service counter and show the redemption code (and pay the redemption price if redeemed via fast track). Once redemption code has been scanned, you may claim your reward. Take note: redemption code cannot be re-used.
What if I changed my mind? I want to replace the item that I recently claimed?
  • Sorry but we will strictly implement the no return, no exchange policy.
  • For more questions, queries, or you just want to say hi, don’t hesitate to email us at stickers@rsci.com.ph.
How can I turn on my GPS to utilize properly the store locator feature of the app?
  • For Android users: Go to settings > Location > Turn on
  • For Apple users: Settings > Privacy > Location Services > Turn on
