Earning stickers

1.
For every purchase of 300php at Rustan’s Supermarket or Marketplace by Rustan’s, you will be entitled to one (1) sticker. A minimum of 100php purchase of sponsor products entitles you to a bonus sticker. However, the number of bonus stickers given cannot exceed the quantity of original entitlement and must not go over 3 pcs. Present your app to claim the sticker/s.
2.
Sticker entitlement that appears on the receipt will always be the basis of entitlement.
3.
App will serve as your sticker card that will be needed for collecting stickers, redemption, and reservation of rewards.
4.
For product refund, the stickers issued for that purchase will be deducted and adjusted in your account.
5.
We have the right to (a) adjust, withdraw or cancel sticker issuance; (b) terminate your membership, at any time at our sole discretion without prior notice if there has been proven foul play and the like.

Transfer of Reward Points to/from another Member
